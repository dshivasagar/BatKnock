🏏 BatKnock Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Summary: BatKnock stores all data locally on your device only. We collect no personal information, use no analytics, and transmit no data to any server.

Introduction

BatKnock is a cricket bat preparation tracker app. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the BatKnock mobile application.

Information We Collect

Data Stored on Your Device

BatKnock stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Bat profiles (name, brand, willow type, size, weight, grains)
  • Knocking session records (knock count, duration, date)
  • Progress data (total knocks, time spent)
  • App preferences (theme, font size)

Microphone Access

BatKnock requests microphone access only during active knocking sessions to automatically count bat knock sounds in real time. We do not record, store, or transmit any audio. No audio data is ever saved.

Camera Access

BatKnock may request camera access to allow you to photograph your bats. Photos are stored locally on your device only.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Location data
  • Financial information
  • Device identifiers
  • Analytics or usage data
  • Any data transmitted to external servers

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers, databases, or cloud storage. Your data never leaves your device.

Third Party Services

BatKnock does not use any third party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

BatKnock is a sports utility app suitable for all ages. We do not collect any personal information from any users including children under 13. The app functions entirely offline with no data transmission.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, security depends on your device's own security features. We recommend keeping your device secure with a screen lock.

Your Rights

Since we collect no personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete beyond uninstalling the app, which removes all locally stored data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
