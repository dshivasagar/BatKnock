## FULL DESCRIPTION (Both stores)
BatKnock is the ultimate cricket bat preparation companion. Properly knocking-in a new bat is essential — and BatKnock makes it effortless and precise.

🏏 AUTOMATIC KNOCK COUNTING
Simply place your phone nearby and BatKnock listens. Using advanced microphone detection, it automatically counts every knock — no buttons, no manual counting. Just knock.

🎯 VOICE REJECTION TECHNOLOGY
Smart calibration filters out background noise, voices and environment sounds. Only genuine bat knocks are counted.

📊 PROGRESS TRACKING
Set knock or time-based targets and track your progress toward a fully prepared bat. Visual progress bars show exactly how far along each bat is.

🔥 HEATMAP & HIT MAP
Visualise your knocking coverage across the bat face. Target specific zones — Sweet Spot, Edge, Toe — and see a colour-coded heatmap showing which areas are well-prepared.

📈 TRENDS & HISTORY
Review all your sessions, track daily knocking activity, and monitor long-term acoustic changes in your bat.

🌙 DARK & LIGHT THEMES
Beautiful dark theme by default with full light mode and automatic system theme support.

📚 BAT CARE LIBRARY
Complete guides for oiling, toe guard application, anti-scuff sheets, storage and crack repair.

✅ OFFLINE FIRST
Works completely without internet. All your data stays on your device.

Perfect for club cricketers, coaches, and serious players who want to get the most from their equipment.
